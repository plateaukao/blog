一出Ifalpes宿舍的門，就可以看到這棵即使下了雪還是滿樹黃葉的大樹。  
  
[![PB154815 (by plateaukao)](/images/blogger/4731464340552338574/2036085976_432fba9201.jpg "PB154815 (by plateaukao)")](http://www.flickr.com/photos/plateau/2036085976/ "PB154815 (by plateaukao)")  
走到它正面來張四平八穩的照片。  
藉由背後的陽光，  
讓葉子自然發亮。  
草地上的白雪也隱約可見。  
  
[![R8061881 (by plateaukao)](/images/blogger/4731464340552338574/2036089252_0bb067cc29.jpg "R8061881 (by plateaukao)")](http://www.flickr.com/photos/plateau/2036089252/ "R8061881 (by plateaukao)")  
低角度來一張，  
展現滿地落葉的氣勢。  
  
  
[![PB154817 (by plateaukao)](/images/blogger/4731464340552338574/2035288261_ed8b9f6b21.jpg "PB154817 (by plateaukao)")](http://www.flickr.com/photos/plateau/2035288261/ "PB154817 (by plateaukao)")  
再低一點，單純拍地上的落葉加白雪。  
  
  
[![R8061890 (by plateaukao)](/images/blogger/4731464340552338574/2035297839_2da35fc4c3.jpg "R8061890 (by plateaukao)")](http://www.flickr.com/photos/plateau/2035297839/ "R8061890 (by plateaukao)")  
低角度拍完了，再加一張白雪特寫。
