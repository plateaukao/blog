Haven't been to my old school for quite a while. Seems everything's changed there. New buildings were built; Roads were transformed into sidewalks with benches here and there. The whole campus is more like a tourist spot during the weekends. Parents will bring their children here to enjoy the spacious squares, especially the one in front of the new library building. The famous festival of Rhododendron that used to only belongs to students are now a public event; each citizens can enjoy the blossoms no matter whether you are studying here or not. Some buildings were like museums, exhibiting different subjects from time to time.

It's great to have a place like this that everyone can escape from the noisy daily life and get a small piece of quietness.
